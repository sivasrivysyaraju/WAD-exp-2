Nykaa, headquartered in Mumbai, India, has emerged as a prominent e-commerce destination for beauty and wellness enthusiasts. Established in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, a former investment banker, Nykaa has grown into a comprehensive platform that caters to diverse beauty needs. The website and app showcase an extensive array of products, including makeup, skincare, haircare, fragrances, and personal care items. Notably, Nykaa collaborates with both international and domestic brands, offering customers a wide selection of high-quality beauty products. Beyond being a marketplace, Nykaa has fostered a community around beauty and self-care. The platform features insightful articles, product reviews, and beauty tutorials, creating an engaging space for users to explore and enhance their beauty routines. Nykaa's commitment to customer satisfaction and its strategic blend of commerce and content have contributed to its success in the competitive beauty and wellness e-commerce landscape in India.
